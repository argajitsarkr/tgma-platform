Pre-allocate tracking IDs for field workers before they go to the field.

Field Worker Workflow
① Before Field Visit
Supervisor allocates a batch of IDs here (e.g., 10 IDs for FW-1, West Tripura Female) and prints the label sheet.
② Physical Labels
Field worker takes the printed barcode sticker sheet. Each sticker goes on the consent form + sample vials for one participant.
③ In the Field (Offline)
Field worker scans or types the pre-printed ID into KoboCollect. No internet or platform access needed.
④ Back at Lab
PI clicks "Sync Now" in KoboToolbox Sync — submissions are pulled in and matched to their tracking IDs automatically.
